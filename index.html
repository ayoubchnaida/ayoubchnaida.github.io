About Me

Hi, I'm Ayoub Chnaida, a dedicated and passionate Data Scientist with a strong foundation in mathematics and a creative flair as an abstract artist.

With over 6 years of experience as a Mathematics Teacher at the Ministry of National Education, I have developed expertise in curriculum development, personalized learning strategies, and fostering analytical thinking among students.

In my academic journey and professional career, I have honed my skills in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data engineering. I am proficient in Python, R, SQL, and MongoDB, and I have applied these skills to projects ranging from customer segmentation to predictive maintenance and deep learning.

Outside of my data science endeavors, I am deeply passionate about abstract art, finding inspiration in the intersection of creativity and analytics. I enjoy exploring new technologies, keeping abreast of industry trends, and integrating innovative solutions into my work.

Feel free to explore my portfolio of projects, Whether you're interested in collaboration or have questions about my work, I'm always eager to connect with fellow enthusiasts in both data science and art!
