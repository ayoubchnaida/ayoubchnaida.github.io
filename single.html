Shneider AIRecruit: A Master Thesis on AI-Driven Solutions for Recruitment

Welcome to the showcase of Shneider AIRecruit, a cutting-edge solution in the realm of Smart Recruitment. This project revolves around leveraging artificial intelligence to revolutionize the recruitment process. By integrating advanced technologies like BERT and Streamlit, Shneider AIRecruit offers powerful capabilities in resume analysis and job matching. Throughout this project, we explore various methodologies in data preprocessing, natural language processing, and machine learning. Our focus includes techniques such as TF-IDF, Word2Vec, and advanced classification algorithms like Logistic Regression and SVM. We also delve into the implementation of BERT for fine-tuning resume classifications and matching job descriptions with resumes using cosine similarity. The application architecture is designed for scalability and efficiency, supported by robust database management and a user-friendly interface accessible to both job seekers and recruiters. Our goal is to streamline the hiring process, enhance decision-making with AI-driven insights, and ultimately facilitate the formation of high-performing teams. Explore the chapters and findings documented in this project, detailing our journey from conception to deployment, key achievements, and future directions. Join us in shaping the future of recruitment technology with Shneider AIRecruit.
